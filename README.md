# Real-Time Marketing Decision Engine

> **Domain:** Marketing Analytics

## Overview

Marketing teams needed to move from weekly, hindsight reporting to rapid, evidence-based decisions grounded in live customer feedback. Current processes were slow, siloed, light on actionable KPIs, making campaign comparison and sentiment shift understanding difficult. Feedback arrived across many channels with inconsistent definitions and ownership. Leaders lacked a shared view of what good looks like, so debates replaced decisions. Without change, organisations risked wasted ad spend, delayed issue responses, lower conversion rates, and missed opportunities to double down on what works.

## Approach

- Ran stakeholder workshops to map decision moments, prioritise use-cases, inventory feedback sources (surveys, chat, social, CRM)
- Defined KPI taxonomy and lifecycle: clear formulas, owners, thresholds, review cadence aligned to marketing funnel
- Modeled unified data, then built near-real-time pipelines to merge feedback, channel performance, spend
- Delivered role-based dashboards with alerts, drill-downs, cohorts, scenario views to guide actions
- Instituted data quality checks, backtesting, A/B readouts to validate KPI stability and reproducibility
- Adopted sprint delivery, playbooks, training; set governance for KPI changes and stewardship
